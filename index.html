FAQ

Answers that matter before a ride gets confirmed.

How do I confirm my booking?

Fill in your trip details, review the fare estimate, and send the booking on WhatsApp for a quick confirmation.

Can distance be calculated during booking?

Yes. For many routes the trip distance can be checked while booking, and you can also enter the distance manually if needed.

What if I do not know the exact distance?

You can still share your pickup and drop points and we will help confirm the route details when replying on WhatsApp.

Is the WhatsApp message detailed enough for confirmation?

Yes. It includes booking reference, trip type, dates, route, cab type, passengers, notes, chargeable distance, and estimated fare.
